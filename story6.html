6.Struggle


Muhammad was already doubtful of his decisions, when suddleny his business went down
Another one of his friend, who he thought so greatly of, ran away with his money
His customers, were not paying for his services
One after another, he was facing problems, greater than the previous one
Having no mental strength, nor time,

he finally gave up on learning, once and for all

Now in his late fifties, Muhammad have a stable business, his children are all well educated and persuing their dream.
But Muhammad still have to look for someone who can read or translates documents to him, although now he is used to it
However, he can't help but wonder, "if I went to school when I was young, will things would be different.
I am sure, I would have avoided those problems and misfortuntes, maybe would be a better version of myself".

Suddenly, he pick up a book, and called his daughter.

It never hurts to try, he thought with a smile.
