4.Grown-ups


Fast forward, Muhammad is now married and now have beautiful three children
He promised himself that his children will go to school, despite whatever tantrum they show
He even managed to create a small business of his own
He soon realises, that his life is now going to throw another series of struggles
Creating a business requires a lot of paper work, reading and understanding

Frustration and anxiety always surrounds him

, making him question his decision
If that was not enough, cunning people, who took advantage of his innocence, ran away with his hard-earned money

"If only I know how to read, this would not have happen",

thought Muhammad to himself sadly.
