3.Desire


Muhammad finally got the chance to go to school, and have some fun,
but sitting on one place for long hours made him think otherwise

One day he ran away from school

and started to play in the farms with the insects
This keep on happening, until one day he was found out

"Oh, so you don't want to study eh?'", asked Muhammad's' father,

"If that is the case, then so be it", said his father

he was send away with his cousin,
to work and learn skills of cutting woods

He never got another chance to go to school, as his parents who themselves never learn,
didn't realised the future they were choosing for him'
