Our Story

At a young age, Muhammad was deprived of education and was force into child labor. His tiny hands never learn how to hold a pen, nor his eyes sparkle reading a story book. The urge to be something, turn him into a hard-working person, who's only aim was to gain money and give-up the idea of learning. With the passage of time, he once again try to open the old chapter of his life, the chapter that was closed for him, from an early age, to learn. But the harsh reality of our society, responsibilities and lack of resources once again made him to give-up on education once and for all. To read the full story Click here

About US

Why Ibtidah?

WHY IBTIDAH? Providing free education to Out of School Children, Adults and Elderly in digitally isolated remote areas around the world. Our vision is to make education a reality for every child deprived of this right. Education can help change the lives of these children and pull them out of child labor, and social ignorance.
